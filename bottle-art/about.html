About My Art

From roadside discarded to gallery-worthy

The Journey of a Bottle

Every piece in this collection begins its journey on the sides of roads — discarded, forgotten, and left to shatter. I collect these discarded brewery bottles and transform them into unique works of art.

Each bottle is carefully cleaned, sanitized, and examined for its unique shape, color, and character. Then through painting, etching, cutting, and assembling, I give each one a new purpose and a new story to tell.

This isn't just art — it's a statement about sustainability, creativity, and finding beauty in what others throw away.

My Philosophy

The values behind each creation

Eco-Conscious

Every piece repurposes glass that would otherwise litter our roadsides and landfills, giving discarded bottles a second life as stunning art.

Handcrafted

Each artwork is meticulously cleaned, designed, and transformed by hand, preserving the natural beauty and character of the original bottle.

Unique Stories

No two bottles are alike, and neither are the artworks. Each piece carries the history of its origin — the brewery, the road, the journey.
